PicLens Qt portable distribution - third-party notices

Qt 6
----
This distribution contains dynamically linked Qt 6 libraries and plugins.
Qt is available under commercial and open-source license options. The license
texts supplied by the Qt packages used to build this artifact are included in
one of these toolchain-specific layouts:

  licenses/qt6-base/
  licenses/qt6-declarative/
  licenses/Qt/
  share/licenses/Qt/
  share/licenses/PicLens/

The Qt libraries remain separate dynamically linked files beside PicLens.exe.

Noto Sans CJK TC
----------------
PicLens embeds Noto Sans CJK TC font files. Their SIL Open Font License text is
included as licenses/NotoSansCJKtc-OFL.txt.
